Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our talent evaluation service

How honest will the evaluation be?

Our scouts provide completely honest, unbiased assessments based on professional standards. They evaluate you as if you were being considered for a professional contract, so the feedback will be direct and constructive.

What information does the scout review?

The scout will review your complete Scylla profile, including all uploaded videos, statistics, achievements, and any additional information you provide. They'll assess your technical ability, physical attributes, mental game, and tactical awareness.

Will this evaluation help me get noticed by scouts?

While this evaluation is for your personal development, having a professional assessment can be valuable for understanding your strengths and areas for improvement. It's designed to help you develop rather than directly secure opportunities.

How long does the evaluation take?

You'll receive your comprehensive 5-page report within 5-7 business days of booking. The scout needs time to thoroughly review your profile and provide a detailed, professional assessment.

Is this suitable for players at any level?

This service is designed for serious players who want professional-level feedback. While there's no minimum playing level requirement, the evaluation will be most valuable for players with competitive cricket experience.
