Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our biomechanics analysis service

Do I need professional camera equipment?

No, a modern smartphone on a tripod is sufficient to capture clear footage. We need videos from side-on, front-on, and behind the stumps for the best analysis.

Is this service suitable if I've had a previous injury?

Yes, it's highly recommended. It can help identify mechanical issues that may have contributed to the injury and provide corrective exercises to prevent recurrence.

How many deliveries should I bowl for the analysis?

We recommend bowling 10-12 deliveries to give us enough material to analyze your action across different deliveries and identify consistent patterns.

Will this help me bowl faster?

Yes, our analysis identifies inefficiencies in your action that may be limiting your pace. The corrective drills are designed to help you bowl faster while maintaining accuracy and reducing injury risk.

What if I'm not satisfied with the report?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your biomechanics report, we'll provide a full refund within 7 days of delivery.
