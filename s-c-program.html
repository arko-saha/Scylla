Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our personalized S&C program design service

How is this different from a generic gym program?

This program is specifically designed for cricket performance, taking into account the unique physical demands of batting, bowling, and fielding. It's tailored to your specific role, age, and goals rather than being a one-size-fits-all approach.

What equipment do I need?

The program is designed to be accessible with basic gym equipment. We'll discuss your available equipment during the consultation and tailor the exercises accordingly. Most exercises can be performed with standard gym equipment.

How long does the consultation take?

The initial consultation typically takes 30-45 minutes. This allows us to understand your current fitness level, goals, available equipment, and specific needs for your cricket role.

Can I modify the program if needed?

The program is designed to be progressive and adaptable. If you have any concerns or need modifications due to injury or schedule changes, you can contact us for guidance on adjustments.

Is this suitable for players at any level?

Yes, the program is designed to be adaptable for players at all levels. During the consultation, we'll assess your current fitness level and design the program accordingly, whether you're just starting out or looking to enhance your existing fitness.
