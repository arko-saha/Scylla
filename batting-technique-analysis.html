Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our video analysis service

What format should my video be?

Standard formats like .MP4 or .MOV are perfect. Most smartphone videos are suitable. We recommend filming from both side-on and front-on (bowler's) view for the best analysis.

Who will see my video?

Your privacy is paramount. Only you and your assigned, vetted coach will ever see your footage. We use secure, encrypted storage and never share your videos with third parties.

What if I am a complete beginner?

This service is perfect for you! It helps build a strong technical foundation from the start. Our coaches are experienced in working with players of all skill levels and will provide appropriate guidance.

How long should my video be?

We recommend 10-15 minutes of footage from a single net or practice session. This gives us enough material to analyze your technique across different shots and situations.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Yes, we offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with your analysis, we'll provide a full refund within 7 days of delivery.
