Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our contract consultation service

What types of contracts can you review?

We can review cricket contracts including county contracts, club agreements, sponsorship deals, and other cricket-related professional agreements. Our specialists have experience with various types of sports contracts.

Is the consultation confidential?

Yes, absolutely. All consultations are completely confidential. Your contract details and personal information are protected under strict confidentiality agreements. We will not share any information with third parties.

Do I need to provide the contract in advance?

Yes, please upload your contract document when booking. This allows our specialist to review it beforehand and prepare specific questions and recommendations for your consultation.

What if I need ongoing legal support?

This is a one-time consultation service. If you need ongoing legal support or contract negotiation assistance, our specialist can provide recommendations for continued legal representation.

Is this service suitable for amateur players?

This service is designed for semi-professional and professional players who are dealing with significant contracts. For amateur players with simpler agreements, we recommend consulting with local legal advice.
