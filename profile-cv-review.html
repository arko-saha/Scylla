Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our profile and CV review service

What does the profile audit include?

The audit covers all aspects of your Scylla profile including photo selection, video content, bio quality, statistics presentation, and overall profile completeness. We'll provide specific feedback on what's working well and what needs improvement.

Do I need to provide my current CV?

Yes, please upload your current CV when booking. This allows our reviewer to assess both your digital profile and offline CV, ensuring consistency and professional presentation across all materials.

What format will the CV template be in?

You'll receive a professionally formatted CV template in both PDF and Word formats, making it easy to customize with your own information while maintaining the professional layout and structure.

How detailed is the feedback?

The feedback is comprehensive and actionable. You'll receive specific recommendations for each section of your profile, including suggested improvements for your bio, advice on video selection, and guidance on presenting your statistics effectively.

Is this suitable for players at any level?

Yes, this service is valuable for players at all levels who want to present themselves professionally. Whether you're just starting out or looking to advance to higher levels, professional presentation is crucial for making a good impression.
