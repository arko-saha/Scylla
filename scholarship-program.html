Are You Eligible?

This is a highly competitive application process. We're looking for the most promising young cricketers with exceptional potential.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 14-18 years old (as of September 1st, 2024)
  • Currently playing at County Age Group level or higher
  • Demonstrated exceptional potential and commitment
  • Active Scylla profile with verified stats
  • Recommendation from current coach or academy
