Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our match day nutrition blueprint service

What information do I need to provide?

You'll complete a detailed questionnaire covering your dietary preferences, food restrictions, current eating habits, match schedule, and performance goals. This helps us create a personalized nutrition plan.

How detailed is the nutrition guide?

The guide is comprehensive, covering pre-match meals (24-48 hours before), match day breakfast, during-match snacks and hydration, and post-match recovery nutrition. It includes specific food suggestions, timing, and portion guidance.

Can the plan accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, absolutely. The questionnaire includes questions about dietary restrictions, allergies, and preferences. Our nutritionists will create a plan that works within your dietary requirements while optimizing your performance.

Is this suitable for different cricket formats?

Yes, the nutrition blueprint takes into account different cricket formats (T20, 50-over, multi-day) and adjusts recommendations accordingly. The plan considers the different energy demands of each format.

How long does it take to receive the blueprint?

You'll receive your personalized nutrition blueprint within 5-7 business days of completing the questionnaire. This allows our nutritionists time to create a comprehensive, tailored plan.
